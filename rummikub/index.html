MINI Rules ☕️

1 The Fast Rule

You may immediately play a tile you just picked from the pile.

2 The Double Rule

At the start of a game, you may announce your doubles and swap them with other players.

3 The Payback Rule

If you provide 2 tiles to a group of 3, you may give any tile from the board to any player — provided you don't break any rule. That player cannot immediately replace that tile where it was.

4 The Odd Rule

Once per game, you may place a run of odds (e.g. 3–5–7) or evens (e.g. 6–8–10).
